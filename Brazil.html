Brazil

Famous Authors

Paul Coelho

After his first few books went largely unnoticed, Paul Coelho gradually rose to international prominence with "The Alchemist". Today, he is considered one of the most influential contemporary authors. He has also made a career as a songwriter, writing for several Brazilian music icons.

Jorge Amado

One of the best known Brazilian authors of the 20th century, Jorge Amado is best known for his books "Dona Flor and Her Two Husbands" and "Teras do sem fim" ("The Violent Land"). He won international acclaim with his stories on life in eastern Brazil, although he originally worked as a journalist in the 1930s and participated in politics, representing Brazil's Communist Party and winning an election to the Constituent Assembly.

Good food

Feijoada

This heart beef and beans stew is Brazil's national dish. Brazil is also well known for its excellent and varied street food, from Pao de quijo (bread and cheese) to Bacalhau (salt cod) bites.
