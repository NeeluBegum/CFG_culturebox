Submit your work!

Have something to contribute? We'd love to hear from you! The guidelines are simple: write a few sentences (ideally a short paragraph or more if you need) about an aspect of a culture you're familiar with. You don't need to be from that country to tell us about it but it'd be great if you have some connection (maybe you speak the language or you love the singer who's from there). Don't forget to include a picture!
