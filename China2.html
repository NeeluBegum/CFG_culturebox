Famous Authors

The Four Classics

The Four Classics are widely considered the basis for and epitome of Chinese literature. The four titles include: "Romance of the Three Kingdoms, "Journey to the West, Water Margin", and "Dream of the Red Chamber".

Good food

饺子 (jiao zi)

Jiao zi are delicious dumplings that are considered a staple at Chinese New Year (but also great year round!). Their fillings can vary but a common one is ground meat and cabbage. Making them is pretty easy and can be tremendous fun! (Note that the picture represents Xiao Long Bao 小笼包, which are filled with a warm soup that explodes in your mouth when the skin of the dumpling is broken.)
