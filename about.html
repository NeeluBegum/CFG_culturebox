    • About us

    If you've ever been introduced to someone from another country and thought "I don't know anything about their country", then you know why we wanted to start The Culture Box.

    The Box helps you learn about a country's famous authors, artists, historians, landmarks, cuisine, and many more. So if you don't know about Senegal's literature scene, this is the place to go!

    We've tried to include as many different sides to culture as possible, from food to famous authors to landmarks. If you want to contribute something about a country you know well, like your favourite singer, we'd love to hear from you! Click on "Submit your work" to find out more about general guidelines and submit your piece.
